# Export Plan

Markdown remains the canonical source for the guide.

## PDF

Target: polished, readable field-manual layout.

Potential requirements:

- Embedded fonts.
- Figures placed near the sections they support.
- Inline citations preserved.
- Optional endnotes or source appendix if the document becomes visually crowded.

## Web

Target: calm single-page or small multi-page site.

Potential structure:

- Landing page with thesis and current draft.
- Sections as navigable anchors.
- Figure gallery.
- Research notes and source appendix.

## Print / zine

Target: compact, tactile guide.

Potential requirements:

- Two-column or booklet layout.
- Simplified figures.
- Pull quotes and field rules as recurring marginalia.
